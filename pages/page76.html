Let us begin by characterizing how the soon-to-be-transformed Foy might go about his journey of gaining a new knowledge of music.

Objective: Listen to influential music.*
Method 1: Find an influential artist and listen to his or her songs, then find another influential artist and listen to his or her songs, and so on.
Method 2: Go to a music store, pick a handful of songs, and listen to them.

We might take Method 1 for granted nowadays because of the proliferation of recommendation engines on Web sites that rarely if ever present content to us unless it’s somehow curated and tuned for our tastes.
